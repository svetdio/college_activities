What is Information Technology

  • In definition from Oxford Languages , Information Technology is the study or use of systems (especially computers & telecommunications) for storing, retrieving, & sending information. ©
  • Information Technology or basically called as IT, are the study of research, design, development, implementation, support, or management of computer-based information systems—particularly software applications & computer hardware. IT experts assist with ensuring that machines are user-friendly.
  • One of the main purpose of IT is to help people to carry out their tasks in easiest & productive way as possible.
